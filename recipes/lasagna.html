Chicken and Spinach Alfredo Lasagna

This easy white chicken lasagna with spinach is a different, yet delicious twist on a classic lasagna!

    Ingredients ½ (8 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ⅛ cup butter
  • ½ tablespoon olive oil
  • ¼ large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 ½ mushrooms, diced
  • ½ cooked rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • and ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ cup ricotta cheese
  • ½ bunch fresh spinach, rinsed
  • ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
